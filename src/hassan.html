Welcome to MCA

Muslim Community Association (MCA) was incorporated as a non-profit religious organization in 1981. We have come a long way from our humble beginnings back in the year 1982-83 when we had a very small place for prayers that soon grew into Masjid Al-Noor.

MCA donor dashboard

Alsalamu Alikom
All MCA community and donors, Jazakom Allah Khair for your Continuous support of MCA.
"It has come to our attention that a couple of our faithful masjid donors are incurring multiple charges on their credit cards due to selected recurring donation payments unintentionally when donating to MCA this Ramadhan. That happened because the donation system by default chooses the recurring donation and the donor has to cancel this choice. We apologize for the inconvenience if you are among the few affected."
We are pleased to offer you some quick solutions to this matter.
1- You can log in to your account from here to view your dashboard and cancel the recurring donation. https://www.mcabayarea.org/donor-dashboard/
 2- Contact via e-mail receipts@mcabayarea.org  for subscription cancellation and refund. 

Jazakom Allah Khair

Our Services
COMMUNITY WELLNESS

MCA wellness committee aims to provide programs and services based on Islamic teachings to build a healthier, stronger Muslim community.

ASK THE SHEIKH

Contact our Shuyookh with any questions you may have. Questions can be sent via email in English or Arabic.

COUNSELLING

MCA is home to Khalil Center offices that provide professional counseling services to the community.

NIKKAH SERVICES

MCA provides the services to perform Aqd or Nikkah for Muslims according to Islamic practice of marriage principles.

FUNERAL SERVICE

MCA funeral services provides support for families of the deceased with all facilities in place for the funeral process.

EDUCATION

We offer a wide variety of educational programs and classes for all ages.

SOCIAL SERVICES

Service to the community is something we strive to uphold. We provide services to the MCA community as well as the community at large through various programs and partnerships

ZAKAH REQUEST
